About

Foodinspector is a mobile app that uses image recognition to help you identify food and determine its shelf life. We aim to reduce food waste by providing accurate and up-to-date information about food expiration dates.

According to the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations, 33% of all food produced for human is wasted globally. This amounts to about 1.3 billion tonnes of food per year. The majority of food waste occurs at the retail and consumer levels, and is due to factors such as expiration dates, spoilage, and over-purchasing.

  • Identifying food by image
  • Determining the shelf life of food
  • Providing tips on how to store food properly

By using FoodInspector, you can help us make a difference.

How we trained our food recognition

Using TensorFlow Transfer Learning an innovative technique, we trained our machine learning model to recognize all types of fruits & vegetables.

Food shelf life data

How does the app know how long food can be stored? Food shelf life data and cooking tips come as open data from the FoodKeeper API. This API is regularly updated and accurate.
